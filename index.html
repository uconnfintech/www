Our Focus? Entrepreneurship.

We believe that entrepreneurship is the key to innovation. UCONN Financial Technology works with students, faculty, and industry to foster entrepreneurship in the Fintech space. Our goal is to make sure that students have the motivation and ability to succeed in Fintech, both by starting their own ventures and participating in other student-led initiatives.
