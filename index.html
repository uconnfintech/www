Spearheading innovation in Financial Technology.

About Us

Started in 2016, the UCONN Financial Technology club exists to spearhead innovation in Fintech.

We believe that entrepreneurship is the key to innovation. The UCONN Fintech club works with UCONN students and faculty, as well as industry, to foster entrepreneurship on UCONN campus. Our goal is to make sure that students have the ability to succeed in Fintech, either by starting their own company or participating in others' ventures.
