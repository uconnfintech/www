Who we Are.

President, Daniel Ruskin:

A Connecticut native, Daniel is a computer science and engineering student at the University of Connecticut. Before attending UCONN, Daniel worked at Coinbase - the leading US Bitcoin exchange - for three years. Now, Daniel is focusing on machine learning with applications in healthcare and financial technology.
