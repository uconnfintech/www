Who we Are.

The University of Connecticut Financial Technology Club is proudly composed of individuals across academic disciplines, and we strive to bring bright and dedicated students into the field of Financial Technology.

President, Daniel Ruskin

A Connecticut native, Daniel is a Honors computer science and engineering student at the University of Connecticut. Before attending UCONN, Daniel worked at Coinbase - the leading US Bitcoin exchange - for three years. Now, Daniel is focusing on machine learning with applications in healthcare and financial technology.

Vice President, Sean Brown

Chief Finance Officer, Jason Ciabattoni

From the Chicagoland area, Jason is a Honors finance major at the University of Connecticut. He is currently the Chief Financial Officer of two undergraduate student organizations. Jason is especially interested in the field of healthcare.

Secretary, Cameron Cantelmo

From Hartford Country, Cameron is an Honors political science and economics major at the University of Connecticut. As a member of UCONN's Special Program in Law, Cameron plans to study how technology will influence financial regulations and the economy as a whole.

Head of Programming and Events,Jack Davis

Hailing from West Hartford, CT, Jack is an Honors electrical engineering major with minors in computer science and math. He is interested in machine learning and controls systems for the purpose of predictive modeling and robotics.
